NOT YOUR AVERAGE DAILY GRIND

We believe mass-produced coffee has lost its soul. At Crank Custom Coffee, we roast specifically for your order. Located in the heart of Calgary, we are a micro-roaster dedicated to the craft.

When you request a roast from us, you aren't just buying a bag; you are commissioning a batch. Every bean is treated with individual attention.

The Science

PRECISION ROAST PROFILING

Roasting coffee is a delicate balance of thermodynamics and chemistry. To ensure your custom batch is perfect, we rely on more than just intuition; we rely on data.

We utilize advanced roasting intelligence software to map the roast curve in real-time. By monitoring the "Rate of Rise" (RoR) and precise bean temperatures, we can manipulate the development of the roast to the second.
